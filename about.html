About CyberPreempt.com

CyberPreempt.com is a strategic digital asset aligned with the emerging preemptive cybersecurity market — a sector identified by Gartner as critical to enterprise resilience in the AI era.

The platform is positioned to support organizations navigating the shift from reactive security to predictive, intelligence-driven defense models.

Market Positioning

Gartner projects that preemptive cybersecurity will represent up to 50% of IT security spending by 2030, driven by AI-enabled threats and expanding global attack surfaces.

CyberPreempt.com was developed to align with this transition, offering category-defining naming, credibility, and strategic positioning.
